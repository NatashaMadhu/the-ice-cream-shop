Why The Ice Cream Shop?

Well, you've scrolled this far and you're obviously craving some ice cream, so why not? And, yes, we do sell ice cream. And dare we say the best darn ice cream you'll ever have! Besides, have you ever heard of an ice cream shop where you can earn and redeem points whenever you stuff yourself with ice cream? We didn't think so! But at The Ice Cream Shop, you can earn Creamies for every purchase and use them to stuff yourself with even more of our heavenly ice cream at your next visit - at no additional cost!
Did we mention we sell ice cream? We think that's reason enough. Happy scrolling!
